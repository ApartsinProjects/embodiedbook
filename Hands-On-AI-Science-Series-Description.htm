Hands-On AI Science is a series of deep, build-it-yourself guides to the major fields of artificial intelligence.

Each book pairs serious scientific depth with practical building. The series develops each field from first principles to the current research frontier, then turns the ideas into runnable implementations, modern library workflows, engineering recipes, exercises, and labs.

The goal is to help readers understand both why AI methods work and how to build systems with them. Core ideas appear first through clear explanations, mental models, worked examples, and from-scratch implementations, then again through the tools and libraries used in real projects.

Each volume is self-contained and course-ready for advanced university study, research seminars, engineering teams, and serious self-study.

Books in the series include:

  • Building Language AI: From Tokens to Agents
  • Building Vision AI: From Pixels to Generative Models
  • Building Temporal AI: From Forecasting to Sequential Decision Making
  • Building Scalable AI: From Big Data Algorithms to Distributed Intelligence
  • Building Embodied AI: From Perception to Autonomous Action
