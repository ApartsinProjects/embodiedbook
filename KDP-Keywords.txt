KDP keyword requirements checklist

- Use up to seven keyword fields.
- Use accurate reader-search phrases.
- Prefer short phrases, usually 2 to 3 words.
- Do not repeat title, subtitle, author names, or category names as filler.
- Do not use HTML tags.
- Do not use quotation marks.
- Do not use claims such as best, bestseller, new, free, or on sale.
- Do not use Amazon program names such as Kindle Unlimited or KDP Select.
- Do not use unrelated brands, other authors, or competing book titles.
- Do not use spelling, spacing, punctuation, capitalization, or plural variants as separate fields.
